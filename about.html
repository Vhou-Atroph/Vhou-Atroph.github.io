Who is Vhou?

Hey, I'm Vhou-Atroph! Feel free to just call me Vhou, (pronounced: voo) though.

I do stuff. A lot of stuff. Some might say too much stuff. But, I enjoy the stuff I do so regardless of how much it may be, I continue to do it. Among this stuff includes programming, making videos, streaming, and writing. I also try to make art sometimes, but I do not consider myself particularly proficient at it. Because of how much stuff I do, I have become very good at finding software that works according to my needs. I have recommended much of these to my friends, and often enjoy hearing about the horrors they encounter while using them.

In regards to programming, my preferred languages are Rust and Python. I have created a couple programs, such as the Toontown Damage Calculator using them together. Since I learned programming primarily through reading books and watching videos, it's been a rough journey. I've learned a lot over time, and I always enjoy learning more!

I took Audio/Video courses throughout high school, and was able to receive certification for Digital Video in Adobe Premiere Pro as a result of it. I even went to university for A/V, but sadly was kicked out because I could not afford to continue taking classes. I do occasionally upload to my Youtube channel, however these videos will generally be low-effort highlights from streams on my Twitch channel.

I've been writing since I was 10- it's always been one of my greatest passions in life. I still don't think I'm fantastic, but I'd like to imagine that I'm alright at it. Among the first things I wrote were Toontown fanfictions. Despite how embarassing it may sound, I really am glad I wrote those stories- they allowed me to express myself creatively, and I learned that I really like writing.
